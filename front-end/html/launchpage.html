Explore Open Educational Resources

Cross-site and cross-lingual recommendations powered by AI. Increase user engagement, grow visibility and drive new audiences, for stronger content consumption and learning at the same time.

X5LEARN is part of the X5GON family of products. It helps OER publishers get the most from their content pages. The recommendation engine is designed to increase content engagement, quality assurance, audience engagement and footfall, directly improving site metrics. X5LEARN's core product is an AI engine connecting millions of pieces of OER content from different sites, cultures and in different languages in one interface based on relevance and personal learning needs. OER publishers use X5LEARN's tools to recirculate their content within other sites, and use it as the first AI powered open education learning environment.

Why choose X5Learn?

Generate Questions

You can automatically create well-written questions by entering your desired text. The website will create multiple questions with different difficulties. You can use this to prepare for a test or learning new information for fun!

Track Your Results

You can easily track your results in order to check how knowledgeable you are in the selected topic. It is also very helpful to understand the parts that needs improvement.

Share Questions

You can share the questions that you generated with your friends! If you are a student, you can compete with your peers for fun while stuyding for your courses. If you are a teacher, you can test your students to see how well they understood that day's lesson. It's up to you!
